Dalol

Dalol is a small town located in the Afar region of Ethiopia. It is known for its unique landscape, which includes the Danakil Depression, a low-lying area that is home to some of the hottest and driest places on Earth. Dalol is also home to the Afar people, who have a long and rich cultural history.

Important Phone numbers in Ethiopia

Hoe To Get To Dalol

To get to Dalol, you can travel by car, bus, or plane. The town is located about 820 kilometers northeast of Addis Ababa and can be reached by driving, taking a bus, or flying to the nearest airport in Mekele and then taking a taxi or hiring a car. It is advisable to plan your trip in advance and book your transportation accordingly, and to be aware of any potential security risks in the Afar region.
