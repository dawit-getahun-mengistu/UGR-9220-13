Timket

Timket is a religious holiday celebrated in Ethiopia that commemorates the baptism of Jesus Christ in the River Jordan. The holiday is marked by colorful processions, music, and traditional dancing, and is attended by people from all over the country. The main celebration takes place in Gondar, a city located in the Amhara region of Ethiopia.

Important Phone numbers in Ethiopia

How To Attend Timket

To get to Gondar for the Timket festival, you can travel by plane, bus, or car. Gondar is located about 740 kilometers northwest of Addis Ababa and is easily accessible from many parts of the country. It is advisable to plan your trip in advance and book your transportation accordingly, especially during peak tourist season.
