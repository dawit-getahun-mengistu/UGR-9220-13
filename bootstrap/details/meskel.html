Meskel

Meskel is a religious holiday celebrated in Ethiopia that commemorates the finding of the True Cross by Empress Helena, the mother of Emperor Constantine the Great. The holiday is marked by colorful processions, music, and traditional dancing, and is attended by people from all over the country. The main celebration takes place in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, at the Meskel Square.

Important Phone numbers in Ethiopia
939 for fire
907 for ambulance service
991 for police
911 for the national emergency number
How To Attend Meskel

To get to Addis Ababa for the Meskel festival, you can travel by plane, bus, or car. Addis Ababa is located in central Ethiopia and is easily accessible from many parts of the country. It is advisable to plan your trip in advance and book your transportation accordingly, especially during peak tourist season.
